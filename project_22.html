Ultimate Beach Abode

Renovate or detonate... this is the question clients are often faced with. In this case, they chose to renovate a 1980's Lockwood home. With an innovative design by Reitsma Architects and some good decisions, the choice was a wise one. The finished product is a contemporary, light filled family home with separate areas for parents, children and guests. Materials include sustainably sourced timbers, concrete, an abundance of glass and a neutral palette of quality tiles, fixtures and fittings.
